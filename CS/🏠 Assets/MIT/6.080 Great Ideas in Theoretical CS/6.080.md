# 6.080 | Spring 2008 | Undergraduate | [Great Ideas In Theoretical Computer Science](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-080-great-ideas-in-theoretical-computer-science-spring-2008/)



## COURSE DESCRIPTION

This course provides a challenging introduction to some of the central ideas of theoretical computer science. It attempts to present a vision of "computer science beyond computers": that is, CS as a set of mathematical tools for understanding complex systems such as universes and minds.
