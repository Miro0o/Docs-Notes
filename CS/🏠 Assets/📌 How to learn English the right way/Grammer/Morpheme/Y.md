	1. conjure
	2. jurisconsult
	3. jurisdiction
	4. jurisprudence, jurisprudent
	5. jurist
	6. adjure
	7. abjure
	8. injure
	9. jural, juridic, juror, jury, nonjuring
	14. objurgate
	15. perjure
3. **just-** = right, 表示“正直，法律”。源自拉丁语 iustus "just."
	1. just, 
	2. justice
	3. adjust
	4. justify
	5. justly
